Our Story

Hot Oven, Local Crowd.

Fired Up Slice Co. started with a simple idea: serve really good pizza to the people we hang out with every day.

Neighborhood First.

We’re not a chain and we like it that way. We know our regulars by name, we remember your usual, and we’re here after the game, after work, and after midnight cravings.

From the music we play to the art on the walls, everything is built to feel like your spot — not some corporate rollout.

What We Care About.

Fresh dough, hot ovens, and a crew that actually loves what they’re making. We mix our dough in-house, grab produce from local suppliers when we can, and keep the menu tight so everything stays dialed in.
